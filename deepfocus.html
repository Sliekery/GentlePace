About DeepFocus

The Pomodoro Technique has helped millions of people stay productive, but most timer apps are cluttered with features you don't need. DeepFocus strips away the noise to give you exactly what matters: a timer, your stats, and nothing else.

The design philosophy is simple - when you're trying to focus, your tools shouldn't compete for attention. DeepFocus presents a clean, calm interface that helps you get into the zone and stay there.

Whether you're deep in code, studying for exams, or pushing through creative work, DeepFocus helps you protect your time and build better focus habits.
