About Budget Room

In a world of streaming services, cloud storage, gym memberships, and countless other subscriptions, it's easy to lose track of what you're paying for. Budget Room was designed to bring clarity to your recurring expenses.

Whether you're managing personal subscriptions or splitting costs with family and roommates, Budget Room gives you the tools to stay on top of your finances without the complexity of traditional budgeting apps.

Built with privacy in mind, all your data stays securely on your device and syncs through your personal iCloud account. We never have access to your financial information.
