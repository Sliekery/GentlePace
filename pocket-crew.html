About Pocket Crew

Ultra running demands meticulous preparation. Between aid station logistics, pacing calculations, mandatory gear lists, and nutrition planning, there's a lot to manage before you even toe the start line.

Pocket Crew was born from countless hours spent with spreadsheets, scribbled notes, and the hard lessons learned from bonking at mile 60. It brings all your race planning into one focused tool.

Whether you're tackling your first 50K or chasing a Western States qualifier, Pocket Crew helps you plan smarter so you can run stronger. And because races happen in remote places, everything works offline once you've set up your plan.
