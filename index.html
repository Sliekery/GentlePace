Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 20, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications created by Tom Graulus (operating as Gentle Pace Apps), including Budget Room, Pocket Crew, and DeepFocus.

Data Collection

Our applications are designed with privacy first. We do not collect personally identifiable information. All user data (such as subscription details, running stats, or timer settings) is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud storage (via Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store).

Third-Party Access

We do not share your data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. Data synchronization is handled exclusively through Apple's iCloud services, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
